Currently the "Buds" as they are sometimes referred to, are 35-14-7 and with the season finished they are on top of their divison and with that comes the chance of playing in the Stanley Cup Playoffs where you face off against other teams in your divison, based on regular season ranking, to try and win the Stanley Cup!

Long time rivals Montreal Canadians are set to face off against the buds in what hopes to be an epic 7 game series with two great teams trying their best and risking it all in hopes to move on to the next round.

Current Stanley Cup Playoff Bracket

Above is the current bracket for the playoffs, there are four separate divisions but only the top four from each division get in. 1st place and 4th place face off against each other and 2nd and 3rd play. As shown above, Toronto is in 1st and are playing their longtime rivals, Montreal Canadians, who are 4th. Winner of this series would then go on to play either Edmonton Oilers (2nd) or Winnepeg Jets(3rd).

Favorite Player

On paper, Toronto has one of, if not the best teams in hockey. They have amazing superstars including Auston Matthews and Mitch Marner, solid goaltending in Frederick Anderson and Jack Campbell, shut down defensive defensemen in TJ brodie and Jake muzzin as well as veterns including Joe Thornton, Jason Spezza and Wayne Simmonds whose not only presence give the leafs the well needed boost to help their game but their grit and determination to help those 3rd and 4th lines thrive.

It's hard to say who my favorite player is on this team as there's an abundance of great and skilled players that i enjoy watching but if there is one i had to pick, it would definitely be Auston Matthews. His mix of speed and skill is almost unmatched through out the league and is no doubt about it, one of the best players to play and only at a young age of 23 means he's only doing to be more dominant in the years to come. He has a knack for scoring and shooting the puck in the net is by far his best feature. Considering how skilled and well prepared the current NHL goalies are makes it even more impressive.

Cup chances for 2021

My expectations for the Leafs to win the Stanley Cup this year aren't the highest. Sure they can probably beat their first round opponents, but after that is their real test. Both oilers and Jets have tremendous teams with both superstar players and elite goaltending and even if they do manage to get past these great teams, they will have to face one of the best two teams in the league, Colorado Avalanche or Vegas Golden Knights. These two teams have it all, superstar forwards, shutdown defensemen who also act as studs when it comes to getting points, and most importantly, the goaltenders. Vegas has Marc Andre Fluery and Robin Lehner who are easily the top goaltending duo in the league while Colorado have Philipp Grubauer and Paval Francouz who has been nothing short of execellent this year. Unfortunatly for us Leafs fan, we haven't been a great playoff team. Regular season yes, but playoffs no. No matter the outcome, i'll always be a leafs fan and will continue to do so with pride, watching my favorite players try their hardest for their chance to host the final trophy, The Stanley Cup.
